As a Communications Manager and blog writer at Global Energy, I was responsible for covering the latest tech news in the energy sphere, conducting interviews with scientists and business leaders, and creating engaging content that highlighted innovations and developments in the global energy sector.

  • Writing articles covering the latest technology news in the energy industry

  • Creating content about energy innovations and sustainable development
