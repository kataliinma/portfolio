Frequently Asked Questions: Billing and Subscriptions

Category: FAQ | Last Updated: December 2024

Find answers to the most common questions about billing, subscriptions, payments, and account management. If you don't find the answer you're looking for, our support team is ready to help.

General Billing Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • Debit cards
  • PayPal
  • Bank transfers (Enterprise plans only)
  • Purchase orders (Enterprise plans only)

When will I be charged?

Billing depends on your subscription type:

  • Monthly Plans: Charged on the same date each month (based on your sign-up date)
  • Annual Plans: Charged once per year on your anniversary date
  • Free Trial: No charge during trial period; billing begins after trial ends

You'll receive an email notification 3 days before each charge.

Can I change my payment method?

Yes, you can update your payment method at any time:

  1. Go to Settings → Billing → Payment Methods
  2. Click "Add Payment Method" or "Edit" next to an existing method
  3. Enter your new payment information
  4. Set it as your default payment method if desired

Your new payment method will be used for all future charges.

Plans and Pricing

What's the difference between the plans?

Here's a quick comparison of our plans:

  • Free Plan:
    • 5GB storage
    • Basic file sharing
    • Web and mobile access
    • Community support
  • Professional Plan ($9.99/month):
    • 100GB storage
    • Advanced sharing features
    • Priority support
    • File versioning
    • Team collaboration tools
  • Enterprise Plan (Custom pricing):
    • Unlimited storage
    • Advanced security features
    • Dedicated support
    • SSO integration
    • Custom compliance features

Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time:

  • Upgrades: Take effect immediately; you'll be charged a prorated amount
  • Downgrades: Take effect at the end of your current billing period
  • Annual to Monthly: Available at the end of your annual subscription

Navigate to Settings → Billing → Change Plan to make changes.

Do you offer discounts for annual plans?

Yes! Annual plans include a 20% discount compared to monthly billing. For example:

  • Professional Monthly: $9.99/month = $119.88/year
  • Professional Annual: $95.90/year (save $23.98)

Educational institutions and non-profits may qualify for additional discounts. Contact us for more information.

Free Trials

How long is the free trial?

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. During the trial:

  • You have full access to all plan features
  • No payment method required to start
  • You can cancel anytime during the trial with no charge
  • You'll receive email reminders before the trial ends

What happens when my trial ends?

If you don't cancel before the trial ends:

  • Your account will automatically convert to the paid plan
  • You'll be charged according to your selected billing cycle
  • You'll retain all data and settings

If you cancel during the trial, you'll revert to the Free Plan and can continue using basic features.

Billing and Invoices

How can I view my billing history?

Access your billing history:

  1. Go to Settings → Billing → Billing History
  2. View all past invoices and payments
  3. Click any invoice to download a PDF copy

You can also request billing history via email by contacting support.

Can I get a receipt for my purchase?

Yes! Receipts are automatically generated for all payments:

  • Email receipt sent immediately after each charge
  • Download PDF receipts from Billing History
  • Request duplicate receipts by contacting support

Do you provide invoices for tax purposes?

Yes, we provide detailed invoices suitable for tax and accounting purposes:

  • All invoices include company details and tax information
  • Enterprise customers can request custom invoice formats
  • Invoices are available in multiple currencies for international customers

Contact billing@cloudsync.com for specific invoice requirements.

Cancellations and Refunds

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Go to Settings → Billing → Subscription
  2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
  3. Confirm your cancellation
  4. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your billing period

Note: Canceling immediately downgrades you to the Free Plan. You'll lose access to paid features but can continue using basic features.

Can I get a refund?

Our refund policy:

  • Monthly Plans: No refunds for partial months; cancel anytime to avoid future charges
  • Annual Plans: Full refund within 30 days of purchase; prorated refunds after 30 days
  • Free Trial: No charge, so no refund needed

To request a refund, contact our billing team with your account details and reason for the refund request.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is safe:

  • All files remain accessible for 30 days after cancellation
  • You can download your data at any time
  • After 30 days, files exceeding Free Plan limits may be deleted
  • You can reactivate your subscription within 90 days to restore full access

We recommend exporting important data before canceling if you plan to switch services.

Storage and Usage

What happens if I exceed my storage limit?

If you exceed your plan's storage limit:

  • You'll receive email notifications at 80%, 90%, and 100% capacity
  • New uploads will be paused until you free up space or upgrade
  • Existing files remain accessible
  • You have 7 days to resolve the issue before restrictions apply

Options to resolve:

  • Delete unnecessary files
  • Upgrade to a plan with more storage
  • Purchase additional storage (Professional and Enterprise plans)

Can I purchase additional storage?

Yes! Additional storage is available as add-ons:

  • Professional Plan: Add 50GB for $2.99/month or 200GB for $9.99/month
  • Enterprise Plan: Custom storage packages available

Go to Settings → Billing → Add-ons to purchase additional storage.

Enterprise Billing

How does Enterprise billing work?

Enterprise billing is customized based on your needs:

  • Per-user or per-seat pricing models
  • Annual contracts with flexible payment terms
  • Volume discounts for large organizations
  • Dedicated account manager for billing questions

Contact our sales team for a custom quote.

Can Enterprise customers pay by invoice?

Yes, Enterprise customers can pay via:

  • Bank transfer/wire transfer
  • Purchase orders
  • Net 30, Net 60, or Net 90 payment terms
  • Multiple payment methods for different departments
