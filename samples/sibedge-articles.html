Category: Content Writing | Role: Digital Communications Manager

About Sibedge

Sibedge is a full-cycle software development company with global reach specializing in helping companies across industries transform their business with the help of technologies. The company provides comprehensive software engineering services, including software production, technical support & DevOps, and software testing & QA.

Role and Responsibilities

As a Digital Communications Manager at Sibedge, I was responsible for creating engaging content that showcased the company's expertise, achievements, and industry insights. My work involved writing articles for the company blog, covering topics ranging from technology trends and industry analysis to company achievements and thought leadership.

  • Writing blog articles on technology trends and industry insights
  • Creating content highlighting company achievements and recognition
  • Developing thought leadership pieces for the software development industry
  • Collaborating with technical teams to translate complex concepts into accessible content
  • Managing content strategy and editorial calendar
