Troubleshooting Common Issues

Category: Technical Support | Last Updated: December 2024

This guide helps you diagnose and resolve the most common issues users encounter. Follow the steps below for each problem, and if you're still experiencing issues, our support team is ready to assist.

Issue: Unable to Sign In

Symptoms

  • Error message: "Invalid email or password"
  • Account appears locked after multiple failed attempts
  • Password reset email not received

Solutions

  1. Verify your credentials:
    • Check that Caps Lock is off
    • Ensure you're using the correct email address
    • Try typing your password in a text editor to verify it's correct
  2. Reset your password:
    • Click "Forgot Password" on the sign-in page
    • Enter your email address
    • Check your spam/junk folder if you don't receive the email within 5 minutes
    • Follow the link in the email to create a new password
  3. Clear browser cache and cookies:
    • Clear your browser's cache and cookies for our site
    • Try signing in using an incognito/private browsing window

Issue: Files Not Syncing

Symptoms

  • Files uploaded but not appearing on other devices
  • Sync status shows "Pending" or "Error"
  • Recent changes not reflected across devices

Solutions

  1. Check your internet connection:
    • Verify you have an active internet connection
    • Test your connection speed at speedtest.net
    • Try disconnecting and reconnecting to your network
  2. Restart the sync service:
    • Close the desktop app completely
    • Wait 10 seconds, then reopen it
    • Check if sync resumes automatically
  3. Check available storage:
    • Go to Settings → Account → Storage
    • Verify you have sufficient storage space
    • Upgrade your plan if you've reached your limit
  4. Verify file permissions:
    • Ensure files aren't locked by another application
    • Check that you have read/write permissions for the folder

Issue: Slow Upload/Download Speeds

Symptoms

  • Uploads taking much longer than expected
  • Download progress bar moving very slowly
  • Timeouts during file transfers

Solutions

  1. Check your internet speed:
    • Run a speed test to verify your connection
    • Compare results with your internet plan's advertised speeds
    • Contact your ISP if speeds are consistently below expectations
  2. Close bandwidth-intensive applications:
    • Pause video streaming services
    • Close other file-sharing applications
    • Disable automatic updates temporarily
  3. Try uploading during off-peak hours:
    • Network congestion can slow transfers
    • Try uploading large files during early morning or late evening
  4. Check firewall and antivirus settings:
    • Ensure our application isn't being blocked
    • Add our app to your firewall's allowed list
    • Temporarily disable antivirus to test if it's causing the issue

Issue: Desktop App Won't Start

Symptoms

  • App crashes immediately upon launch
  • Error message appears when trying to open
  • App icon appears in taskbar but window doesn't open

Solutions

  1. Restart your computer:
    • Sometimes a simple restart resolves temporary issues
    • Ensure all app processes are closed before restarting
  2. Reinstall the application:
    • Uninstall the current version from Control Panel (Windows) or Applications (Mac)
    • Download the latest version from our website
    • Install the new version and sign in again
  3. Check system requirements:
    • Verify your operating system is supported
    • Ensure you have sufficient RAM and disk space
    • Update your operating system to the latest version
  4. Run as administrator (Windows):
    • Right-click the app icon
    • Select "Run as administrator"
    • This may resolve permission-related issues

Issue: Mobile App Notifications Not Working

Symptoms

  • Not receiving push notifications
  • Notifications appear delayed
  • No sound or vibration with notifications

Solutions

  1. Check app notification settings:
    • Go to Settings → Notifications in the app
    • Ensure notifications are enabled
    • Verify specific notification types are turned on
  2. Check device notification settings:
    • iOS: Settings → Notifications → [App Name]
    • Android: Settings → Apps → [App Name] → Notifications
    • Ensure notifications aren't blocked at the system level
  3. Restart the app:
    • Force close the app completely
    • Reopen and sign in again

Still Need Help?

If none of these solutions resolve your issue, please contact our support team with the following information:

  • Description of the problem
  • Steps you've already tried
  • Screenshots or error messages (if applicable)
  • Your operating system and app version
  • Any recent changes to your system or network

Our support team typically responds within 24 hours and can provide personalized assistance.
